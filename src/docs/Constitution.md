### 1.1 🧠 THE ORACLE (The Semantic Nervous System)
* **Definition**: The authoritative repository of world knowledge, material definitions, historical lore, spatial taxonomy, and relationship states.
* **Sovereignty**: Owns all taxonomical metadata definitions, catalog schemas (`MI_`, `TEX_`, `GEO_`), and lore boundaries.
* **Prohibitions**: The Oracle is strictly passive. It **MUST NEVER** execute runtime gameplay mechanics, authorize economic transactions, mint tokens, or modify active character state vectors.
* **Core Duty**: To provide objective semantic meaning to the platform.

### 1.2 🛡️ THE SENTINEL (The Governance Engine)
* **Definition**: The runtime integrity, authentication, validation, and security monitoring authority.
* **Sovereignty**: Owns the anti-cheat subsystems, transaction verification nodes, cryptographic integrity signatures, wallet bindings, and Operator trust indexes.
* **Prohibitions**: The Sentinel **MUST NEVER** invent lore, redefine material properties, or generate procedural terrain/architecture.
* **Core Duty**: To protect the platform and ensure deterministic compliance with system rules.

### 1.3 🌍 WGE (The World Generation Engine / World Synthesizer)
* **Definition**: The procedural layout generator, terrain synthesizer, and chunk-streaming driver.
* **Sovereignty**: Owns the geometric deformation, parametric structures, station meshes, layline network routing, and chunk instantiations from seed parameters.
* **Prohibitions**: WGE **MUST NEVER** invent its own lore, persist unauthorized economy states, or bypass Sentinel verification checks.
* **Core Duty**: To instantiate the semantic definitions of the Oracle as real, traversable geometric and environmental structures.
